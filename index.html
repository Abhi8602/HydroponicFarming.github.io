About Hydroponics

  • Hydroponic System is a system of growing crops without soil, often called soilless farming.
  • In the hydroponic system, the plant roots grow in a liquid nutrient solution or inside the moist inert materials like Rockwool and Vermiculite.
  • The liquid nutrient solution is a mixture of essential plant nutrients in the water.
